Why based on camera?

Since the CMOS is getting cheaper and cheaper, cameras, especially cameras on phones become a low cost device which can be accessed by anybody. With the support of powerful libraries for phones using IOS and Android operating systems, apps on phone are able to provide a cheap and convenient solution for the computer vision based fruit grading.

Techniques used for camera based fruit grading

Generally, the camera and image based method has two steps, the first step is to segment the image from the background, and the second step is to classify the fruit quality level based on the segmented image.
